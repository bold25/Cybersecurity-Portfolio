Botium Toys Security Audit

 Overview

The Botium Toys Security Audit project aims to evaluate the security posture of Botium Toys, focusing on identifying vulnerabilities, assessing compliance with relevant regulations, and providing actionable recommendations to enhance overall security. The audit is structured to assess the effectiveness of current security controls and outline areas for improvement in order to mitigate risk and enhance the safeguarding of sensitive data.

Purpose and Objectives

The key objectives of this audit include:

- Assessing the effectiveness of Botium Toys' existing security framework and controls.
- Identifying gaps in compliance with industry standards such as PCI DSS and GDPR.
- Formulating a strategic plan to improve the security infrastructure and ensure data integrity, confidentiality, and availability.
- Providing a comprehensive final report detailing findings and recommendations for enhancing security measures.

Methodology

The audit follows the National Institute of Standards and Technology Cybersecurity Framework (NIST CSF) to structure our findings and recommendations. The approach utilized includes:

1. Scope Definition: Outlining the assets managed by the IT department, including employee equipment, on-premises systems, internal networks, and data management practices.
2. Risk Assessment: Evaluating potential risks associated with asset management and compliance with U.S. and international regulations.
3. Controls Assessment: Analyzing the existing administrative, technical, and physical controls in place, followed by a compliance checklist that adheres to relevant standards (e.g., PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC).
4. Recommendations: Developing actionable recommendations to address identified deficiencies and improve security posture.

How to Navigate the Repository

This repository is organized to provide a clear structure for accessing all relevant materials related to the audit:

- audit_report/: Contains the final audit report detailing findings and recommendations.
- checklist/: Includes the completed controls and compliance checklist for Botium Toys.
- risk_assessment/: Contains documents summarizing the risk assessment conducted during the audit.
- supporting_materials/: Offers critical documentation and reference materials utilized throughout the audit process.
- presentations/: Contains any presentation files made to communicate findings to stakeholders.

Getting Started

To explore the repository, simply navigate through the folders listed above. Each folder contains relevant files that provide insights into specific components of the audit process. You can download, review, and, if necessary, contribute to the documentation and findings by creating pull requests.
